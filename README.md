2. Short Description / Purpose  
The Finance KPI Dashboard is a visually engaging, dynamic and analytical Power BI report designed to help users explore and compare the performance of a chocolate company over sales persons, team and a particular time period. The Dashboard focuses on highlighting major business KPI's like Target Sales, Actual Sales, Variance, Trend and Target Met Indicator.

5. Features / Highlights  
Business Problem:  
The Awesome Chocolates Company generates millions in revenue, but are not being able to hit target in some months, teams. Thus to compare the target retention across time period, sales person and sales team is indicated in this dashboard.  

Goal of the Dashboard:  
Goal of the dashboard is to analyse the sales of each team, sales person, month to check whether the sales target is met in the given fields or not. The secondary goal of the dashboard is to show the major KPI's like revenue, sales, target sales, actual sales, variance, variance% and monthly sales trend of the company.
